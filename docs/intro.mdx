# Getting Started with JerryBB

JerryBB is an assistant manager, scout, and analyst for BuzzerBeater. It brings together your team's data, opponent preparation, tactical history, player development, and match analysis so you can make better decisions before and after games.

## What JerryBB helps you do

- Prepare for an upcoming opponent.
- Review how your team has performed under different tactics.
- Inspect opponent tactical patterns across multiple games.
- Understand player development, roster performance, and team context.
- Review what happened in a completed match.

## The main product loop

Before a match, start at **Home** and open the next opponent's **Briefing**. Use the briefing for the immediate matchup, then consult **Tactical History** when you need to understand whether the opponent's behavior is a recurring pattern rather than a one-game observation.

After a match, use **Match Analysis** to review that specific game. Return to **My Team Tactical History** or the opponent's history when you want to compare patterns across multiple games.

## Important distinction

Single-match preparation and tactical history answer different questions:

- **Briefing:** What should I consider for this upcoming game?
- **Tactical history:** What patterns have appeared across previous games?

Historical evidence can inform preparation, but it does not replace matchup-specific analysis.
