# My Team Tactical History

My Team Tactical History examines your team's past tactical choices and outcomes across multiple games.

## What this page is for

Use it to understand how your team has performed under different tactics and to provide historical context for future preparation.

## Decisions supported

This history can help you investigate:

- Which tactics your team has used most often.
- How results differ across the tactics in the available history.
- Whether a result is supported by enough games to be useful.
- Which team-level patterns deserve deeper lineup or player analysis.

## How to interpret the history

Separate what happened from what it means. A win or loss is an outcome, not by itself proof that a tactic caused the result. Review sample size, opponents, lineup context, and data completeness before treating a pattern as actionable.

## Difference from Match Analysis

Match Analysis explains one completed game. My Team Tactical History compares multiple games to reveal broader patterns.
